Who is Zeus

Image sourced from: www.inthefieldstories.net

Zeus is the God of the sky and controls thunder and lightning. Zeus is considered the king of the Gods due to his incredible power and status as the only child of Cronus to fight back. Zeus has four children with his wife Hera: Ares, Eileithyia, Hebe, and Hephaestus.
